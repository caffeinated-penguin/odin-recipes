Mediterranean Bowl

Description

This is the perfect recipe for a warm summer day. It's fresh, flavorful, and will have your friends and family asking for more.

Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs beef chuck roast
  • 2 red onions, quartered
  • 8 garlic cloves, peeled and whole
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 bunch mint
  • 1 package grape or cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 4 oz feta cheese
  • 3 tbsp unflavored greek yogurt
  • 12 oz beef broth
  • Seasonings: salt, pepper, paprika, cumin, onion powder, oregano

Steps

  1. Place the meat in a large Pyrex dish.
  2. Season both sides of meat with the spices. Rub in well.
  3. Pour in 12 oz beef broth. Add red onion and 6 of the 8 garlic cloves.
  4. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and place in the oven at 350 degrees fo 3 hours.
  5. Remove the dish from oven. Shred and combine the meat with the drippings in the dish, then place back in the oven for another 45 minutes to 1 hour. Shred again when you remove from the oven.
  6. While the meat cooks, chop the vegetables and the mint.
  7. Make the rice.
  8. To make the sauce, in a small food processor or blender add the yogurt, feta, 2 remaining garlic cloves, a handful of mint, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Optional: For a thinner sauce, add milk or almond milk.
  9. Complete the bowl by adding first rice, then meat, and then fresh vegetables and mint. Drizzle sauce on top.
