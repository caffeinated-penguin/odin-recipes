Miso Sesame Dressing on Broccoli Salad Recipe

Description

Craving a spring side dish that's healthy, unique, and filled with unforgettable umami flavor? Look no further. We've got you covered.

Ingredients

Body

  • 2 heads of broccoli
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1 bunch fresh mint
  • 1 head of romaine
  • 1 bunch mint

Dressing

  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 heaped tsp miso paste
  • 4 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut broccoli into florets, mix with oil and bake at 360 degrees F for 20 min.
  2. Finely chop romaine, cilantro, and mint.
  3. Mix the roasted broccoli with the chopped ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Combine dressing ingredients and whisk until well combined.
  5. Pour dressing over salad, toss together, and enjoy!
  6. Remove the dish from oven. Shred and combine the meat with the drippings in the dish, then place back in the oven for another 45 minutes to 1 hour. Shred again when you remove from the oven.
