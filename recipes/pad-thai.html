Pad Thai

Description

This recipe is always a crowd-pleaser. Who needs take-out?

Ingredients

Body

  • 2 x 16 oz bags, flat rice noodles
  • Vegetable oil
  • 10 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 red bell peppers, cut into strips
  • 6-8 chicken thighs, cut into small chunks
  • Green onions (garnish)
  • Cilantro (garnish)
  • 4 limes (garnish)
  • 4 oz feta cheese
  • 3 tbsp unflavored greek yogurt
  • 12 oz beef broth
  • Seasonings: salt, pepper, paprika, cumin, onion powder, oregano

Sauce

  • 12 tbsp fish sauce
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 8 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 4 tbsp sriracha
  • 6 tbsp peanut butter
  • 6 tbsp brown sugar

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions, drain, and rinse under cold water
  2. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a wok.
  3. In the wok, cook chopped chicken thighs with salt and peper until seared and browning.
  4. Add red bell pepper and cover wok partially with lid to help red bell peppers soften.
  5. Add garlic once everything in the work is mostly cooked.
  6. Scramble and add in eggs.
  7. Fold in the cooked noodles.
  8. Prepare sauce and whisk to combine. Add sauce to the wok.
  9. Top your pad thai with cilanto, green onions, and fresh lime juice.
  10. While the meat cooks, chop the vegetables and the mint.
